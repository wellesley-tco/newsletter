🎥 Music Video Features

  • South Side - 頑童 MJ116
    Are you into rap? How about Mandarin rap? MJ 116 is a group of Taiwanese rap pioneers who helped sow the seed of hip hop throughout Taiwan and China. They started with Southern Style music with a strong rhythm and rap flow, which at the time was a complete breakthrough in the old school Mandarin Music scene. Their strong rhythmic style led them to become a major favorite in Taiwanese clubs. South Side is one of their major hits and offers a vivid depiction of Taiwanese street culture.
